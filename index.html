Learn About Us

14 Years Experience

Booysen Accountants, founded by Johan Booysen in 2011, is a SAIPA-registered practice dedicated to excellence in accounting, taxation, and payroll services. With offices in Pretoria, Gauteng; Greytown, KwaZulu-Natal; and Jacobs Bay, Western Cape, we leverage modern technology to deliver personalised services across South Africa and abroad.

Our team, driven by Johan’s entrepreneurial spirit and strategic experience, is committed to building strong client relationships and providing innovative, cost-effective solutions tailored to your business needs. From monthly accounting and cloud system implementation to tax planning and business valuations, Booysen Accountants is your trusted partner in achieving business success. Join us and experience the future of accounting today.
